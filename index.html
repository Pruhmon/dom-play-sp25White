Assignment Overview:

This page is part of an interactive project where we explore DOM manipulation in HTML and CSS. The main goal is to dynamically display a dialogue from a scene, with each character's lines tagged with a data-actor attribute. This allows us to easily apply styles or interact with the page based on the characters or actions.

The project involves understanding how to structure and manipulate content, as well as how to leverage custom attributes to represent metadata for each element. In this case, the page displays lines from a script with characters speaking in a play or performance, making it a useful tool for simulating a script reader or an interactive story.

Script Interactive:

MOHAMED: So... you’re Xenu. The infamous space overlord of galactic imprisonment?

XENU (smirking): And you're Mohamed. Prophet of the desert. Messenger of divine order. We’ve both got titles.

MOHAMED: Titles mean nothing without truth. What exactly do you offer your followers?

XENU: Enlightenment through science and spirit. Freedom from trauma. We unlock the Thetan within.

MOHAMED: With e-meters and fees? Truth shouldn’t require a credit card.

XENU: And what do *you* offer? Prayers? Submission? Rules written in the sand?

MOHAMED: Discipline. Unity. Compassion. A way of life grounded in service and sincerity—not galactic warfare and volcanoes.

XENU: Humans need spectacle to believe. Stories that blast through the mind like comets. I give them cosmic scale.

MOHAMED: And I give them purpose. A connection not to memory implants, but to each other. To something greater than either of us.

A pause. Xenu studies him, floating ever so slightly above the ground, cape billowing in silent wind.

XENU: Perhaps... your methods work for Earth. But the stars still remember me.

MOHAMED: Let them remember. I’d rather be written in hearts than on cosmic prison walls.

They stare at each other—two leaders, two legacies. One grounded. One adrift in space.

XENU (grinning): We should do this again sometime.

MOHAMED (smiling slightly): Only if you come back down to Earth.
