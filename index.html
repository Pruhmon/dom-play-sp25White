Assignment Overview:

This page is part of an interactive project where we explore DOM manipulation in HTML and CSS. The main goal is to dynamically display a dialogue from a scene, with each character's lines tagged with a data-actor attribute. This allows us to easily apply styles or interact with the page based on the characters or actions.

The project involves understanding how to structure and manipulate content, as well as how to leverage custom attributes to represent metadata for each element. In this case, the page displays lines from a script with characters speaking in a play or performance, making it a useful tool for simulating a script reader or an interactive story.

Script Interactive:

LORD KFC: So, Xenu, we’ve gone from interstellar prisons to soul-scanning gadgets. Still trying to sell people salvation through sci-fi?

XENU: And you're still pushing ancient rituals and bearded obedience? At least I let people level up.

A glowing portal suddenly opens mid-air, slurping with garlic-scented steam. PRAMAN THE SCHOLAR steps out, robes flowing like boiling pasta water.

PRAMAN: Gentlemen, please. You squabble over outdated doctrines and space-drama. Have you considered... the Flying Spaghetti Monster?

XENU (raising an eyebrow): The what?

LORD KFC: Is this a cooking class or a prophecy?

PRAMAN: Neither. It’s enlightenment through carbs. Love, randomness, and meatball-based metaphysics. We call it “Pastafarianism.”

XENU: This is absurd.

PRAMAN: And yet... no wars were ever started over penne.

LORD KFC (chuckling): He’s got a point.

XENU: No, what he has is a food blog disguised as a belief system.

PRAMAN: Wrong. We believe in logic *and* laughter. In questioning everything. Even marinara.

Lightning cracks in the background. A meatball rolls slowly across the stage. Silence follows.

LORD KFC: You know what? Between laser volcanoes and spaghetti, I’ll take the sauce.

PRAMAN: R’amen, brother.

XENU (sighs): I traveled galaxies for this?

They all stare at each other. Somewhere in the distance, a colander glows on a mountaintop.
